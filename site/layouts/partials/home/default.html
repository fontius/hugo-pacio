Pacio Blockchain  |  API Protocol

Accounting and management

Pacio is an applications development and deployment platform that extends decentralised technologies to make them suitable for widespread use by applications, wherever the apps run. Pacio adds broad accounting, management, and semantic data functionalities that are interoperable across entities, jurisdictions, standards, apps, and blockchains, plus a cryptocurrency with a purpose, comprehensive governance capabilities, and an ecosystem of tools and services.

Zero transaction costs

Pacio will help developers of consumer or personal applications create apps which provide end user benefits of more security, control over personal data, and zero transaction costs, thereby facilitating better alternatives to current centralised web systems, and opening up new market segments such as zero cost remittances, micropayments, and data exchange/markets.

More profitable business outcomes

Pacio will help developers of business applications create more competitive and useful apps which provide the consumer benefits above for the customers of a business running the app, and for the business itself, help produce more profitable outcomes through processing of business activity more comprehensively with full accounting for more insightful semantic analysis and reporting, including in real time.

Future proof blockchain governments

Pacio will help developers of applications for charities, NGOs, governments, regulators, foundations etc create more useful apps which provide the consumer benefits above for the users of the entity running the app, and for the entity itself, help improve its management and operation through processing of activity more comprehensively with full accounting for more insightful semantic analysis and reporting.

Pacio Technology

Pacio will use a third generation blockchain for the security, immutability, and cryptocurrency strengths of blockchain technology, but without restricting use of Pacio to applications running on the same blockchain. Given the explosion of blockchain technology options available in 2018, Pacio has no intention of developing its own blockchain, but will work with one of the alternatives.
