Bringing Businesses into the 21st Century

Pacio.io brings Semantic Web for Business Data. It allows for easy exchange of accounting and controlling data worldwide. Pacios Blockchain solution enables global accountability – the foundation upon which all economy is built.

The challenge: setting standards to end all fraud

Today it is rather easy to commit tax-fraud because there is no global standard to follow the money. Even an US-American corporation finds it difficult to exchange accounting data with it’s own British subsidiary. The incompatibility comes from countries and corporations creating Data silos. Pacio connects every single one with it’s groundbreaking program.
Read more about the global challenge and the Pacio use-cases

Pacio tools to set a global business data standard

1. The Pacio blockchain brings security, immutability, incorruptibility and auditability to business data and allows inter Blockchain operations

2. The Standardised Semantic Information Model (SSIM) stores and standardises Data across companies, jurisdictions and accounting standards

3. Single Entry Accounting Done Right! (SEA) is an improvement of the 500-year-old book-keeping tradition that brings accounting into the Blockchain-age

4. Target Average Rate Index (TARI) is well proven method to provide real-time business insight that enables controlling when it is needed: now
Read more about the tools in the Pacio ecosystem

Pio: The token that fuels Pacio

Pacio is an ecosystem for application developers and corporations. They can access the methods via APIs and integrate Pacio into their own developments. The motor for this ecosystem is Pacio’s own Pio-Token.

The token allows:
• to use Pacio services
• as a stake in the governance of the Pacio ecosystem
• a cryptocurrency in it’s own right with unique benefits

Pacio – the brainchild of leading accountancy pioneer David Hartley

David Hartley

Australian David Hartley has not just seen it all – he has been where computer history was made: he invented a computer plus an operating system in the mid 1970ies and pioneered the first digital accounting software in the 1980ies. In the 2000ies he grew a multi-million Pound Software company and is currently jumping the Blockchain bandwagon: “I have been tinkering about Pacio for 40 years but only now – with the invention of Blockchain – has the time come to great global business accountability”, says Hartley.
Read more about David Hartley.

Dr Keith N. Cleland

The professor at the IBR School of Executive Management at Steinbeis University Berlin has invented a revolutionary method to get real-time business insight into corporations with the Target Average Rate Index (TARI). The method allows executives to get clarity of their bottom line within a few minutes with a single Key Performance Indicator. TARI is used in numerous small and medium-sized businesses.
